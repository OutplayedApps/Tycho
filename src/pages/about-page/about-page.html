About Us What is Tycho? What is quizbowl? What is science bowl? Tycho is a mobile app that allows people to practice for academic competitions such as Science Bowl and Quizbowl.
Science Bowl is a buzzer-based competition sponsored by the U.S. Department of Energy conducted at the local, regional, and national levels.
Quizbowl is a buzzer-based competition, but it is based on longer, pyramidal questions in many different academic categories such as science, literature, history, philosophy, geography, music, and more.

Note: This app is not affiliated with the U.S. DOE or other quizbowl organizations. The science bowl questions are freely available on the public domain from the DOE website.
The quizbowl questions are used from the QuizDB database, which contains sets posted publicly on the Quizbowl Resource Center. What is "Reader" and "Game" mode? Reader mode is meant for people reading questions to others; it is perfect for use during practices or mini-tournaments.
Game mode is meant for if you are playing by yourself and want to have some fun practicing yourself! Why was it made? We are former science bowl and quizbowl players who made this app to make it easier for teams to study and practice! Learn more about us at outplayedapps.com.

Our goal is to provide a convenient way to access both quizbowl and science bowl questions offline and/or on the go.
This app also includes exclusive science bowl questions made by science bowl alumni, from sample RR (round robin) and DE (double elimination) rounds.
