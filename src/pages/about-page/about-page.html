About us What is Tycho? Tycho is a mobile app that allows people both to access packets and to access study guides for academic competitions such as quizbowl. Why was it made? We found that downloading quizbowl packets from the website is not a very easy experience, especially on mobile phones, as one has to open .docx files and unzip archives. Tycho contains all the quizbowl packets in a user-friendly, easy-to-use way. Additionally, it may be hard to start out as a new quizbowl player without some guidance as to what to study and how to study it. That's what we hope our guides are useful for! Why is the app so large? It contains all the packets from quizbowlpackets.com for offline viewing. Perfect for use on a car ride or flight! Who made this? This app was designed and made by some former members of the Chattahoochee High School quizbowl team. Ashwin Ramaswami and Abhinav Kumar first came up with the initial concept, and Ashwin is the lead developer.
Study guides are made by various quizbowl players and former quizbowl players.
