About Us What is Tycho? Tycho is a mobile app that allows people to practice for academic competitions such as Science Bowl. What is "Reader" and "Game" mode? Reader mode is meant for people reading questions to others; it is perfect for use during practices or mini-tournaments.
Game mode is meant for if you are playing by yourself and want to have some fun practicing yourself! Why was it made? There are sample questions available on the DOE website, but this app provides a convenient way to access them offline, on the go. We are former national science bowl players who made this app to make it easier for teams to study and practice! Learn more about us at outplayedapps.com. I'm a developer. Where's the code? You're in luck! The app is open source and developed using the Ionic framework, a cross-platform mobile app development platform that uses Angular 2. You can contribute to it at our Github repository.
